Our Vision

Our History

2011 - The Beginning

We began our journey as Sai Goods Carrier with a single truck, initially serving only the Haryana region. This marked the start of our commitment to reliable logistics.

2020 - Regional Growth

We expanded our operations into Punjab, Himachal, UP, and Rajasthan, significantly increasing our fleet to meet growing demand.

2025 - Continued Growth

With a fleet size of over 100+, 7 branches, and 300+ service locations, we continue to expand our reach and services.
